21 Bridges

Chadwick Boseman plays a police officer working for the NYPD in search of ther truth, which requires him to close off 21 Bridges in or out of the city.

Chadwick Boseman plays a police officer for the NYPD who is a detective trying to uncoverr the truth as to who killed 5 police officers.Two men try to complete a robbery of cocaine from a storage house. Both men get into a shootout with NYPD, they escape on foot and are trying to make it ouut the city. As Boseman continues his investigation he begins to wonder who the real criminals and requests all 21 bridges in or out of NY city be closed off. He later uncovers a sinister secret inside the NYPD. This action thriller shows Boseman in a different light, he plays a serious no nonsense detective who feels like the NYPD is against him. In this film he shows how he mastered the fighting choreography needed to perform and his tone of voice and emotion make you belive he is that detective.
